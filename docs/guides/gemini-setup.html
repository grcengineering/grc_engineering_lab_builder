Follow these steps to use your generated prompts with Google Gemini

Step 1: Start a New Chat

  1. Go to gemini.google.com
  2. Start a new chat

Step 2: Paste Both Prompts

  1. First, paste your System Prompt (the first prompt from the generator)
  2. Press Enter and wait for Gemini to acknowledge it
  3. Then paste your User Prompt (the second prompt)
  4. Press Enter
  5. Gemini will generate your personalized learning lab!
